Our Story

About the Institution

🏫

Kusum Bikash Vidyapith is a well-established private educational institution founded in the year 2006. The school offers quality education in the Bengali medium, catering to students from KG I to X. Since its inception, the institution has been committed to nurturing young minds by providing a strong academic foundation along with moral values and discipline.

With a student-focused approach, Kusum Bikash Vidyapith aims to create a supportive and engaging learning environment where every child is encouraged to grow intellectually, socially, and emotionally. The school emphasizes holistic development through a balanced blend of academics, co-curricular activities, and character building.

Over the years, the institution has earned the trust of parents and the community by maintaining consistent educational standards and fostering a culture of excellence. Kusum Bikash Vidyapith continues to strive towards shaping responsible and confident individuals who are prepared to face future challenges.

Course of Study

The school follows the curriculum prescribed by the Board of Secondary Education, Assam (SEBA). The syllabus is designed to ensure a strong academic foundation and systematic learning for students from KG I to Class 10, in accordance with SEBA guidelines.

Class VI–VIII Middle school — structured academics
Class IX–X Secondary level — SEBA Board examination
